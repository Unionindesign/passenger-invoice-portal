User Stories

Group business has gone up by 20% since last year. This is in large part due to the new trend towards the 'non-traditional' group with no dedicated group leader. Groups of travelers are learning that if they can get a 8-10 people together, additional discounts make the trip more viable for everyone. Rather than having a travel agent or group leader handle the entire reservation, the trend is towards managing each person within a group on an individual basis, while offering the same level of group discounts.

Currently, only one type of invoice is available. This invoice is worded towards travel agents, or someone who is prepared to manage 'commisions', 'clients,' and 'referal fees.' We need to be able to easily split the group credit, and send invoices direct to all members in a group, whether or not they are getting any of the group discounts, without having to take additional steps to hide information on the current invoice templates.
